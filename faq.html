FAQ

  • 1. What is NeverBeLonely? NeverBeLonely is a social app designed to help people meet new friends in real life. It simplifies the process by removing the need for swiping, messaging, and awkward small talk.
  • 2. How does it work? Simply create a profile, upload a picture, select your available time, and press the button. The app will match you with other users in your area who are also looking to meet.
  • 3. Do I need to swipe or chat with people before meeting? No, the app removes the traditional swiping and chatting process. You get matched automatically and meet directly at a well-known location.
  • 4. Where do the meetups happen? The app suggests trusted, public locations in your city where you and your matches can meet.
  • 5. Can I choose who I meet? Matching is based on your selected time and location. You do not manually pick who you meet, making the experience organic and natural.
  • 6. How many people can I meet at once? Each meetup includes up to 3 people, creating a comfortable group dynamic. This helps increase the chances of forming a connection and makes the experience safer, especially for women who may prefer group settings.
  • 7. Is NeverBeLonely a dating app? No, it is not a dating app. The goal is to help people find meaningful friendships and social connections without the pressure of dating.
  • 8. Is the app free to use? Yes, the basic features of the app are free. There may be premium options in the future for enhanced experiences.
  • 9. How is my data protected? We prioritize privacy and security by following best practices and industry-standard safeguards to protect your personal information.
  • 10. What if I want to delete my account? Currently, account deletion is not available. However, you can contact our support team to deactivate your account if needed.
  • 11. How do I contact support? You can reach our support team via email only.
